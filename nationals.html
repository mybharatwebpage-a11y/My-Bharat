National Pledge

India is my country. All Indians are my brothers and sisters.
I love my country and I am proud of its rich and varied heritage.
I shall always strive to be worthy of it.
I shall respect my parents, teachers and all elders,
and treat everyone with courtesy.
To my country and my people, I pledge my devotion.
In their well-being and prosperity alone lies my happiness.

Indian National Flag ‚Äî The Tiranga

Meaning of colors & Ashoka Chakra:
  • Saffron: Courage and sacrifice
  • White: Peace, truth, and light (with Ashoka Chakra)
  • Green: Fertility, growth, and auspiciousness
  • Ashoka Chakra: 24-spoke wheel representing Dharma (righteousness)
Quick Flag Code Tips:
  • Proportion: 3:2 (length:height)
  • Do not let the flag touch the ground; handle with respect.
  • Do not use the flag as clothing that disrespects it.

Indian National Calendar (Saka Era)

The Saka calendar is the national civil calendar used alongside the Gregorian calendar.
